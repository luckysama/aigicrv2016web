Registration Instructions

The registration and payment are handled through University of Victoria's Conference Management Services. The above link will open a page with a list all possible registration types. After you navigated to the Registration Services webpage:
  1. On the first page, select the registration type that fits you, and click the green "Register" button on the bottom of the page.
  2. On the second page, you can pick additional reception tickets and banquet tickets. After that, please fill up the User Account form about your information. Required fields are:
    • The Username and Password are necessary for creating an account in the system, and you may review your registration later by using this username/password pair.
    • Make sure the Name, Affiliation and Email are correctly filled in.
    Then click the green "Register" button to the next step.
  3. The next page prompts you that you will be taken to the University of Victoria's payment link. The payment link will show that you are paying to University of Victoria Library's Conference Services. The NOTE field of the payment form and the receipt will show that the payment is for AIGICRV.
  4. After successfully pay your registration, please print your receipt for future references.

If you filled in your information but the payment is unsuccessfully or interrupted:

You can login into the system with your account on the right sidebar from the registration link. There is no need to refill the User Account form if you already have an account. Personal information can be edited from the "My Profile" link on the right sidebar once you log in.

If on-line credit card payment is not possible, and other forms of payment (cash, cheque, etc.) are needed:

Directly contact the registration manager: aigicrv2016 at uvic dot ca

If cancellation and refund are required:

Directly contact the registration manager: aigicrv2016 at uvic dot ca
The registration includes 15% Tax and 10% service fee. Cancellation and refund can be made upon request but the tax and service fee will not be refunded.

Membership Information

To register with the member rate, a membership in your participating conference is required. The membership will be checked upon your arrival at the conference venue, but not the time of your registration. The membership requirements of the three conferences are listed below:

Artificial Intelligence

Membership of the Canadian Artificial Intelligence Association (CAIAC) is required.
Membership subscription can be purchased at :
https://www.caiac.ca/user/register

Note that AI conference registration makes you a member of CAIAC for 1 year. So attend the AI/GI/CRV conference last year will automatically entitle you for the membership discount this year.

Graphics Interface

Membership of the Canadian Human-Computer Communications Society (CHCCS) is required.
Membership subscription can be purchased at :
http://graphicsinterface.org/chccs-scdhm/membership/

Computer and Robot Vision

Membership of the Canadian Image Processing and Pattern Recognition Society (CIPPRS) is required.
Membership subscription can be purchased at :
http://www.cipprs.org/register.html
