Accommodation


There are two places reserved for your stay for the 2016 AIGICRV Conferences. Either stay on the campus of University of Victoria with a walking distance to the conference venue, or book a room at the side of the beautiful inner harbour in down town. As a famous tourist destination, there are a lot of other hotels around Victoria down town. Since May is a busy tourist season, we recommend to book early to ensure room availability.

University of Victoria Residence Service

University of Victoria Residence Service provides on campus accommodation. Call 1-250-721-8657 and ask for AI/GI/CRV CONFERENCE group rate to book.
Details below.

Victoria Marriott Inner Harbour

The Hotel of Marriott Victoria Inner Harbour is located in Victoria down town at 728 Humboldt Street. Call 1-866-306-5451 and ask for AI/GI/CRV CONFERENCE group rate to book.
Details below.

University of Victoria Residence Services

http://www.uvic.ca/residence/visitors/index.php

 The university residence services provides on-campus accommodation with walking distance to the conference venues. Bookings and inquiries can be made by emailing to bookings@uvic.ca or call 250-721-8657 and mentioning the AI/GI/CRV conference.

We also have on campus food services and parking lots around the residence buildings to make your stay comfortable!

Victoria Marriott Inner Harbour

http://www.marriottvictoria.com/

 The Victoria Marriott Hotel is located on the side of the beautiful inner harbour in Victoria down town, with walking distance to the attractions, dining and shopping places in the city centre. Hotel room can be booked at Toll Free: 1-866-306-5451 and mentioning the AIGICRV conference. On-line reservation can be made at http://www.marriottvictoria.com/ with the group code AGCAGCA/ AGCAGCB/ AGCAGCZ A pre-configured booking link can be found here.
