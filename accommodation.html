Accommodation


There are two places reserved for your stay for the 2016 AIGICRV Conferences. Either stay on the campus of University of Victoria with a walking distance to the conference venue, or book a room at the side of the beautiful inner harbour in down town. As a famous tourist destination, there are a lot of other hotels around Victoria down town. Since May is a busy tourist season, we recommend to book early to ensure room availability.

University of Victoria Residence Services

http://www.uvic.ca/residence/visitors/index.php

 The university residence services provides on-campus accommodation with walking distance to the conference venues. Bookings and inquiries can be made by emailing to bookings@uvic.ca or call 250-721-8657 and mentioning the AI/GI/CRV conference.

We also have on campus food services and parking lots around the residence buildings to make your stay comfortable!
There are four options for on-campus accommodation: The Craigdarroch Guest House provides a hotel-like experience, while the Dormitory, Cluster and Apartments are similar to student residences; some of them have kitchens. Breakfast is included with certain kinds of reservations, but food can always be conveniently purchased from the Cadboro Commons dining hall, located at the centre of these on-campus residences. Please visit the Housing Service's official webpage (http://www.uvic.ca/residence/visitors/index.php) for more information.

Victoria Marriott Inner Harbour

http://www.marriottvictoria.com/

 The Victoria Marriott Hotel is located on the side of the beautiful inner harbour in Victoria down town, with walking distance to the attractions, dining and shopping places in the city centre. Other accommodation options


Please note that the conference management is NOT in contact with any of these hotels/B&Bs described in this section. Book with your own discretion.


As a world-class tourist destination, down-town Victoria hosts a plethora of hotels ranging from the prestige Fairmount-Empress Hotel (http://www.fairmont.com/empress-victoria/) to the budget option of Days Inn (http://www.daysinn.ca/hotels/british-columbia/victoria/days-inn-victoria-on-the-harbour/). There are many convenient buses routes and taxis run between the university and city centre.

There are also some off-campus local B&Bs with walking distances to our university, located in a quiet corner of the beautiful city. Cadboro Bay Bed and Breakfast (http://www.bbvictoria.ca/) and Arbutus Haven Bed & Breakfast (http://www.arbutushaven.com/) are the closest places around our university.
