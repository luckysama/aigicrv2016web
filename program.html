Program Schedule

This is a tentative schedule and may be revised before the conference starts.
The three conferences are allocated in three buildings in University of Victoria. Except for the welcome session and the social events, GI is in the Bob Wright Centre (BWC) room A104 and (mostly) B150. CRV is in MacLaurin Building (MAC) room A144, and AI is in David Strong Building (DSB) room C103. The conference management is in BWC. Please see the Travel page for detailed directions of getting around the campus.
